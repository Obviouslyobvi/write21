Core Principles

⏱️

Use Timers

Set 10-minute timers per task to avoid perfectionism and maintain momentum

🚀

Keep Moving

Don't polish as you write. Speed over perfection in the first draft

✍️

Write First, Edit Later

Complete the manuscript before worrying about quality

💬

Write How You Talk

Use simple, clear, direct language. Don't try to sound "bookish"

📊

Know Your Market

Publishers want moneymakers. Understand your audience size and demand

🎯

Structure First

Outline completely before writing content. It becomes "fill in the blanks"
