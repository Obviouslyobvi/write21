Foundation: Brain Dump First

This is the first day of the 21-day Nonfiction Blueprint system. You'll go from scattered ideas to a complete book outline in one sitting. The key: dump everything out of your head before trying to organize it.

Step 1

Say Your Affirmation

Set a timer for 1 minute. Say aloud: "I am now a writer. I am an author. I am focused on the tasks I have to do today, and I eliminate all excuses and distractions."

Step 2 — The Most Important Step

Brain Dump Everything

Get paper and pen. As quickly as possible, from memory, list everything a person needs to know about the subject of your book. Single words or phrases ONLY. Do NOT write sentences. Do NOT worry about order. Just write.

Step 3

Organize Into Chapters

Number your items in a logical order. Each item becomes a chapter title. You just created your table of contents. Many items may become sub-chapters nested inside other chapters.
